Proprietary Systems (IP Core)

Our work ships as standards, not one-offs. Each project couples a concrete deliverable with a durable method.

  • Orquesta Inmóvil™ — a unified scoring model deriving all cues from one invariant orchestral body for tonal and diegetic coherence.
  • Breath Track™ — psychoacoustic conductor realism via breath-based rubato and timing cues.
  • Performance Encryption™ — embedded interpretive nuance layers that resist reverse-engineering.
  • Destructive Environment Test™ (DET™) — resilience protocol to validate score survival under hostile playback conditions.
